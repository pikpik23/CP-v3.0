CMAPS Operating instructions:

Resetting the device

  • Press both of the reset buttons (one silver, one red) when the device is powered on.
  • Pay attention to the blue light near the reset button,
  • If the light is constant the device is initialising, give it some time.
  • As long as the blue light is flashing, check the LCD screen to see whether it has a lock or is currently acquiring one.

Care and management of the Device

 

  • Where to store the devices
    • In webbing
    • Away from direct sunlight
    • Away from water
    • Away from items that may hurt the device e.g. Food
    • Ensure that the device is either kept upright
  • Treat the CMAPS unit with care and caution, as you would with your own phone. As it costs over $300.

Acquiring a signal

  • Turn on the device and hold the outer button.
  • The LCD should glow and display 1 of the following 3 items:
    • displaying latitude and longitude coordinates,
    • displaying no lock
    • and/or not displaying anything or stange text.

If the device displays coordinates then it is successful!

If the device does not display coordinates then follow the following procedure.

Acquiring A Signal “No Lock”

If the device displays “No Lock” follow the following steps

  • The antenna is plugged in and facing the sky
  • You have a clear line of sight with the sky
  • The device is turned on
  • Try resetting the unit

Acquiring a Signal (No Text/ Strange Text)

 

  • If no text or strange text is being displayed then:
    • Unplug and replug the arduino
    • Press the small button on the corner of the arduino
    • Reset the device
  • If the problem still persists, it is most likely a code issue so a new unit should be requeste

Acquiring a signal (Can’t acquire a signal)

  • If the CMAPS device is unable to acquire a signal, refer to the CMAPS troubleshooting guide.
  • If acquiring a signal take an extended period of time (e.g. 10 minutes), open the case and search for the blinking blue light.
  • If the light is blinking at a fast pace (few times a second) it has a lock/is acquiring a lock.
  • If it is blinking but not displaying a set of coordinates on the LCD screen, reset the device.
  • If the light is blinking slowly try the troubleshooting methods.
  • NOTE: The area around ØB has bad reception, therefore your CMAPS unit may not connect as easily, still however go through the troubleshooting guide.
