Training aims to adopt a greater understanding by people in the operating and running of the CMAPS device.

Training exercises to teach others how to use CMAPS for the website

  • CMAPS EX, where a group goes to different locations in the school to teach people how to get the longitude and latitude, and also to make sure it works.
  • Lost exercise, where a platoon is “lost” and so then have to find there way back to HQ with the CMAPS unit, a map and compass and a radio.

  • How to turn the Unit on
    • Open the unit housing.
    • Connect the USB cable to a power pack.
  • How to turn the Unit off
    • Hold down the power button on the battery pack or
    • Unplug the Usb cable from the battery
    • Check that the LCD screen is no longer on
